Welcome to Black Dice!

Here is how to play Black Dice and win the game:

  • Click the "New game" button to start a new game
  • The input bar will show up, type in the number of players that are playing and click "Submit"
  • When it's your turn click on the "roll" button to roll the die and it will display the side you got.
  • The number displayed gets added to your total score and the first player to 21 wins
  • However if you roll a "1" you automatically lose
  • A couple of handy shortcuts: press spacebar to start a new game and enter to submit number of players
  • Finally, have fun!
